About Us

After graduating Godfrey Sammut worked for a leading audit firm for over 3 years. He subsequently occupied various senior positions in industry. In 1990 Godfrey Sammut set up his practice which is allied to other local legal, accountancy and audit practices.

With a wide client base operating in various sectors of the economy, the firm is a small and medium entity specialist, providing its services to limited liability companies, partnerships and self-employed.

Godfrey Sammut is a Fellow of the Malta Institute of Accountants and an Associate Member of the Malta Institute of Taxation.
